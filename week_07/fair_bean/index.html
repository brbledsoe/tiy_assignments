About

Fair Bean Coffee is located in what has been termed ‘South Austin’s Coffee District’. We have free wireless internet, couches, plenty of seating and lot’s of sunlight. As we roast coffee beans in our store daily, our dining room has the delicious smell of freshly roasted coffee beans. Feel free to come, drink a cup of our delicious coffee and enjoy watching south austin whiz by our floor to ceiling windows.
